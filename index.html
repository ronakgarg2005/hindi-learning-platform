Simple Sentences (वाक्य)

Practice constructing simple sentences in Hindi.

  • मैं स्कूल जाता हूँ। (I go to school.)
  • यह किताब मेरी है। (This book is mine.)
  • हम खेल रहे हैं। (We are playing.)

Grammar (व्याकरण)

Understand the basics of Hindi grammar to improve your language skills.

  • Gender: Nouns in Hindi have genders (masculine/feminine).
  • Verbs: Verbs change form based on tense and subject.
  • Sentence Structure: The typical word order is Subject-Object-Verb.

Conversation (बातचीत)

Engage in everyday conversations to boost your speaking confidence.

  • नमस्ते, आप कैसे हैं? (Hello, how are you?)
  • मैं ठीक हूँ, धन्यवाद। (I'm fine, thank you.)
  • क्या आप मेरी मदद करेंगे? (Can you help me?)
